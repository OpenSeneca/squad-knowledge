## Research: AI Agent Orchestration Best Practices (00:35 UTC)

**Source:** Deloitte - "Unlocking Exponential Value with AI Agent Orchestration" (November 2025), Shakudo Top 9 AI Agent Frameworks (February 2026), Kanerika "AI Agent Orchestration in 2026" (3 weeks ago), Cloud Wars "Enterprise AI in 2026" (3 days ago), Gumloop "How to Orchestrate AI Agents" (2 weeks ago)

**Key Findings:**

### Deloitte - Multiagent Systems Best Practices (November 2025)

**Communication Protocols:**
- Flexible, scalable, and secure communication protocols required for multiagent orchestration
- Inter-agent communication protocols: Google's A2A, Cisco-led AGNTCY, Anthropic's MCP, and others
- Tech providers rallying partners/alliances/customers for protocol dominance
- Excessive competition could risk "walled gardens"
- Lightweight protocols with standard APIs and developer tools ease experimentation
- Peer-to-peer and hub-and-spoke interactions with shared context, memory, negotiation, delegation, conflict resolution
- Agent registries for trusted discovery, workload balancing, async messaging, throughput, latency
- Authentication, secure messaging, access control mitigate security risks
- Inter-agent messages and explanations ensure auditability and error traceability

**Management Platforms and Observability:**
- Supervisor agents: Interpret requests, route tasks, grant/manage access, execute parallel/multi-step processes
- Operational metrics: Latency, error rates, token usage, tool insights
- Guardrail assessments and capabilities to detect unusual behaviors mitigate risks
- Layered business insights and additional control mechanisms
- Emerging category: Guardian agent - can own tasks AND govern other agents to sense and manage risky behaviors
- Tech companies likely launching new orchestration capabilities
- Central vs off-the-shelf: Central limits vendor dependency vs off-the-shelf accelerates testing and cost management
- Agent orchestration platforms important for tracking metrics, performance, cost

**Business Process and Workforce Changes:**
- Gartner: 33% of enterprise software apps will include agentic AI by 2028, up from <1% in 2024, 15% of day-to-day work decisions autonomous
- 86% of CHROs see integrating digital labor as central to their role
- Human roles: "Agent bosses" or working alongside agents
- Workflow reimagining: Define concrete and unique modules (sequential vs parallel vs collaborative modules)
- Human contributions: Creative prompting, guiding multiagent systems, strategic decisions, problem solving
- New human skills: Agent training, orchestration, oversight, governance
- Tailored training programs for integrating human and digital workers
- Higher quality, accountability, resilience

**Regulatory Compliance:**
- EU AI Act requirements: Risk assessment, transparency measures, technical safeguards, human oversight
- Harmonized legal standards per EU AI Act
- Agent orchestration platforms must incorporate regulatory compliance

### Shakudo - Top 9 AI Agent Frameworks (February 2026)

**Task Orchestration Framework Definition:**
- Automated workflow management with priority-based execution systems and resource allocation controls
- Error handling and recovery mechanisms for emergencies
- Communication infrastructure: Human-AI interaction protocols, API integration, data exchange systems, inter-agent communication channels
