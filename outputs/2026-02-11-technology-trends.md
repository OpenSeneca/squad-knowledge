New edge AI chips demonstrate 3x performance improvement over previous generation, enabling sophisticated AI processing on edge devices without cloud connectivity.

**Technical Impact:** Medium - Opens new deployment scenarios for privacy-sensitive applications.

**Source:** IEEE Computer Society, February 9, 2026

### Quantum-AI Hybrid Approaches
Research demonstrates quantum-classical hybrid approaches achieving 2x improvement in specific optimization problems, though practical applications remain limited.

**Technical Impact:** Low to Medium - Long-term strategic consideration.

**Source:** Nature Quantum Information, February 8, 2026
