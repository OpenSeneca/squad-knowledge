**Regulatory Impact:** High - Affects product development and market access.

**Source:** EU Commission Press Release, February 9, 2026

Commerce Department expands export controls on advanced AI chips and AI development tools, targeting specific countries and use cases.

**Trade Impact:** Medium - Affects international operations and partnerships.

**Source:** Commerce Department Announcement, February 8, 2026

NIST releases updated AI risk management framework with new guidelines for AI deployment in critical infrastructure.

**Standards Impact:** Medium - Provides compliance benchmarks for industry.

**Source:** NIST Publication, February 7, 2026
